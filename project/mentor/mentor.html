About Me

* I am Ms. Ashwitha C Thomas, serving as an Assistant Professor Grade-I in the Department of Information Science & Engineering at NMAM Institute of Technology, Nitte. With a deep passion for teaching and academic excellence, I am dedicated to fostering an engaging and inclusive learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, explore innovatively, and grow confidently.

* I strongly believe in the power of continuous learning, both inside and outside the classroom. My academic journey and professional experience have shaped me into an educator who values not just knowledge delivery but also mentorship and guidance. I am also actively involved in research, and one of my notable contributions includes being a co-inventor of the Smartphone Based Indian Sign Language Recognition System, aimed at enhancing accessibility through technology.

* Through my teaching, research, and collaborative work, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving field of Information Science and support the holistic development of my students.
